About Us

Welcome to our website!

Credit card fraud detection is presently the most frequently occurring problem in the present world. This is due to the rise in both online transactions and e-commerce platforms. Credit card fraud generally happens when the card was stolen for any of the unauthorized purposes or even when the fraudster uses the credit card information for his use.


In the present world, we are facing a lot of credit card problems. To detect the fraudulent activities the credit card fraud detection system was introduced. This project aims to focus mainly on machine learning algorithms. The algorithms used are random forest, Nearest Neighbours, LGBM, Decision Tree. The results of these four algorithms are based on accuracy, AUC-ROC Score. All the four algorithms are compared and the algorithm that has the greatest accuracy is considered as the best algorithm that is used to detect the fraud.
